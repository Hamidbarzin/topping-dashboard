ماده ۱: مقدمه و رسالت

شرکت تاپینگ کوریر بر اساس متد NAS™ (که نماد شبکه‌سازی هوشمند، به‌کارگیری هوش مصنوعی، تضمین امنیت و سرعت است) بنیان گذاشته شده و به عنوان یک شرکت بین‌المللی در حوزه لجستیک و فریت فورواردینگ، متعهد به رعایت بالاترین استانداردهای جهانی است.

ماده ۲: اصول بنیادین متد NAS

  • شبکه‌سازی هوشمند (Network): ایجاد شبکه گسترده و یکپارچه از نمایندگان در سراسر جهان که همگی بر اساس اصول NAS عمل می‌کنند. این شبکه باید مانند یک خانواده جهانی، با همدلی و هماهنگی کار کند.
  • هوش مصنوعی (AI): استفاده از فناوری‌های پیشرفته هوش مصنوعی برای بهینه‌سازی مسیرها، افزایش دقت و سرعت خدمات و ارائه تجربه برتر به مشتریان.
  • امنیت و سرعت (Secure & Swift): تضمین امنیت امانات مشتریان و ارائه خدمات با بالاترین سرعت ممکن برای حفظ اعتماد و اعتبار بین‌المللی.

ماده ۴: سیستم نظارت و کنترل کیفیت

  • AI Monitoring Center: نظارت ۲۴/۷ بر عملکرد در زمینه سرعت، کیفیت و رضایت مشتری
  • Smart Contract System: قراردادهای هوشمند برای تضمین شفافیت و امنیت

ماده ۵: سیاست برند و انحصار

  • تخطی از سیاست برند منجر به فسخ قرارداد و پیگرد قانونی خواهد شد

Article 1: Introduction & Mission

Topping Courier is founded on the NAS™ Method (symbolizing Intelligent Networking, Artificial Intelligence Implementation, Security Assurance, and Speed), and as an international company in the logistics and freight forwarding sector, is committed to maintaining the highest global standards.

Article 2: Fundamental Principles of NAS Method

  • Intelligent Networking: Creating an extensive and integrated network of agents worldwide who all operate based on NAS principles. This network should function like a global family, working with empathy and coordination.
  • Artificial Intelligence: Utilizing advanced AI technologies to optimize routes, increase accuracy and speed of services, and provide superior customer experience.
  • Secure & Swift: Ensuring customer shipment security and delivering services at the highest possible speed to maintain international trust and credibility.

Article 3: Agent & Hub Obligations

3-1. General Requirements

  • Complete adherence to NAS principles in all activities
  • Mandatory participation in Topping Academy training courses
  • Monthly performance report submission by the 5th of the following month
  • Participation in quarterly technical and ethical evaluations

3-2. Performance Metrics

Article 4: Monitoring & Quality Control System

  • AI Monitoring Center: 24/7 monitoring of performance in speed, quality, and customer satisfaction
  • Smart Contract System: Smart contracts to ensure transparency and security
  • Global Compliance Engine: Automatic compliance with export, import, and transportation laws of each country
  • Quality Assurance Team: Quality assurance team for surprise and periodic inspections

Article 5: Brand Policy & Exclusivity

  • Each country will have only one official NAS™ hub
  • Each hub can have a maximum of 10 licensed local agents
  • Use of brand, logo, or official domains without written permission is prohibited
  • Violation of brand policy will result in contract termination and legal action
ℹ Note: Brand usage permission requests must be submitted to headquarters at least 30 days before use.

  • Regular algorithm auditing to prevent discrimination
  • Transparency in automated decision-making

7-3. Confidentiality of Business Information

Confidential: Disclosure of company, customer, or competitor business information is prohibited and subject to legal action. Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) is mandatory for all agents.

Article 9: Dispute Resolution & Legal Matters

9-1. Dispute Resolution Mechanism

Stage 1: Direct negotiation between parties (maximum 15 days)

9-2. Governing Law

This contract is subject to the laws of the United Arab Emirates (company headquarters) and Dubai courts have jurisdiction.

9-3. Contract Termination Conditions

  • Mutual Termination: With 90-day written notice and settlement of all debts
  • Unilateral Termination by Company: In case of serious contract violation with 30-day notice
  • Immediate Termination: In cases of fraud, information disclosure, or serious brand damage

9-4. Consequences of Termination

After contract termination, the agent must:

  • Deliver all shipments in process
  • Remove all brand-related content from online platforms
  • Return all documents, equipment, and access received
  • Observe non-compete commitment for 12 months in the same geographical area
